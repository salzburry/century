## Task

This task is split into several sections. Please answer each question under its section.

1. **Data Assessment**
    1. Draw a diagram showing how the datasets link together.
2. **Data Cleaning**
    1. The data may be messy and require cleaning. Note a few issues you identified and fixed across the datasets.
    2. Note that the data file types are different across the files.
3. **Descriptive Analysis**
    1. How many distinct patients are in the dataset?
    2. Plot distinct medications over time using a plotting library. If you find duplicates, revisit your cleaning step.
    3. Create a pie chart showing the percentage of patients by race and gender.
    4. What percentage of patients have all four symptom categories ≥ 30?
4. **Descriptive Analysis**
    1. Write a short analysis plan describing how you will examine how combinations of treatments and other variables relate to outcomes (defined by symptoms).
        - For example, you might explore ethnicity and age groups across therapies and time frames and examine how outcomes change. Pick a few cohorts, and feel free to be creative!
    2. Conduct the analysis described above and describe your results! Be as detailed as you can, and include visualizations
5. **Statistical Analyses**
    1. Build a regression model predicting whether an outcome measure (e.g., Fatigue) decreases after a chosen time period following a medication. Feel free to decide which variables and time window to use.
    2. Provide a brief interpretation of the model output, including how to interpret coefficients and significance.
